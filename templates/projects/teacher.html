Teacher Formation & Time Redemption

Teachers are at the heart of education. Yet too often, they are crushed under administrative burdens, endless grading, and fragmented resources. Our Teacher Formation & Time Redemption initiative seeks to free teachers from busywork so they can focus on their true calling: forming students in truth, virtue, and wisdom.

Through workshops, micro-grants, and carefully designed tools, we help teachers reclaim planning time, deepen pedagogy, and be refreshed in their vocation. This project equips them not only with methods but with spiritual encouragement to endure in the good work of teaching.

Your support provides training sessions, curated resources, and practical automations that multiply the time teachers have to invest directly in students.
