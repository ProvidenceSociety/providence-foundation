Who We Are

Providence Foundation is a Christian nonprofit dedicated to renewing education—uniting classical wisdom with carefully designed technology. We exist to help families, churches, schools, and educators cultivate disciplined minds, virtuous character, and a lifelong love of learning.

What We Do

  • Curriculum & Pilots: Support classical and faith-aligned learning pilots that combine rigorous content with modern delivery.

  • Teacher Formation: Provide training, grants, and resources that redeem time and equip teachers to flourish.

  • Student Access: Fund scholarships and local initiatives that open doors for underserved learners.

  • Responsible Innovation: Partner with trustworthy builders to ensure technology serves human formation—not the other way around.

Our Aim

Transforming education, redeeming time, and reframing technology for the next generation—to the glory of God and the good of neighbors.
